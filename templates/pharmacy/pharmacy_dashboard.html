About

Medicare is a web-application which customers can order their medicine by uploading the picture of prescription and get the home delivery of medicine by cash on delivery. Medicare solves the problem for people who lives in remote areas. The people will get the service available at any time the customer can order their medicines in 2 ways: By uploading the picture of prescription and By searching for a medicine. Our aim is to provide medicine to patients at the doorstep and aware customers for online medicine availability.
